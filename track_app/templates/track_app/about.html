Track Light

The goal of TrackLight is to present internet users with the various tracking services embedded on websites. Whether it is tracking software from Google, Adobe, Facebook, or others, users of the net have a right to know who is watching.

Tracklight currently scans sites for the presence of the top 500 or so tracking companies. More info about the trackers can be found here.

TrackLight is fairly simple under the hood. The app scans entered URLs for trackers in both the site's source HTML and the network calls made by the site.

For more information on trackers, please check out whotracks.me.
